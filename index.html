About TGS 100

TGS 100 is TiE Global’s premier annual awards program for early stage technology startups. This prestigious event offers global startups the chance to showcase their products, network with investors, and connect with industry leaders.

Recognizing top technology and technology enabled startups based on their IP, business model and traction, TGS100 provides unparalleled exposure to venture capitalists, angels, and entrepreneurs.

Join us for an opportunity to pitch your startup to 300-500 investors, mentors, and TiE Charter Members from around the world, and take your business to the next level.

*Selected startups will pitch on Dec 10 at the TiE Global Summit ’24 and have the opportunity for live Q&A.

How Do I Qualify?

Companies must be privately held and of good legal standing

Companies must be between 2 months to 5 years old

Companies must be looking to raise INR 1 - 10 Crores

Companies must not have raised more than INR 1 crore of external capital thus far

Who Are The Judges?

Judges’ identities, their affiliations, company representations, scoring patterns, and all information related to their participation in the TGS 100 selection process are confidential. Each external judge will work independently and will not know the scores or identities of the other judges. External judges are selected only by invitation and are subject to TiE ’s strict confidentiality and disclosure guidelines.

What is the jury looking for in the winners?

The jury will be looking for investable companies which can achieve VC scale. They will be looking for:

Strong founding team with an unfair advantage

Strong product differentiation & deep moat

Large market and clear GTM plan
